Impact in Laos

Before becoming the first CSO advocating for LGBTQIA+ rights in Laos, we were the first to organize IDAHOBIT in 2015 and since then, we have garnered more and more support from international entities, local officials and companies. With this, we were able to undertake LGBTQIA+ research projects, conduct peer education for big corporations and produce resources that can be used to further raise awareness and inspire inclusivity.
